ABOUT US

What is NCAlinked ?

NCALINKED is a virtual learning platform for NCA students which allows student to take online courses and deeply understand what they learned in school. Students who find their focus suffering from classroom activity may benefit from online classes and, Students who aren’t as assertive may have better opportunities to participate in class discussions.

What are the features ?

NCALINKED IS VERY USER FRIENDLY. It is very easy to setup. (it takes less than 1 minute to setup and start learning).Filtering courses by searching their name or choosing a category. The courses are given by videos and each video have a link that allows user to refer to the pdf file that the teacher is teaching in. It is free! Any students can take the course for free, which increases their knowledge and confidence. It doesn’t only have to be courses taken in school. You can take courses about programming, general knowledge and many others.

OUR GOALS

Creating a smart NCA students community . The students won’t be confused when learning a new topic because they have a concept of what they will be learning in the next class. It makes teaching easier for teachers in NCA because the students have an idea of what they are learning. Increases class participation and class discussion.
